How It Works

3 simple steps to a better business

If you can use WhatsApp, you can use Karigaar. No training. No tech skills needed.

01
📲
Download & Set Up in 10 Minutes

Install Karigaar on your phone. Add your business name, your team members, and your services. Done. No training required — it's as simple as setting up a new phone.

02
📋
Assign Jobs & Watch Your Team

When a customer calls, create a job in seconds and assign it to your technician. See live updates as they start, work on, and complete each job — from your phone.

03
🧾
Bill Instantly & Get Paid Faster

Job done? Generate a professional GST bill in one tap. Send it on WhatsApp immediately. See who paid and who hasn't — no register, no manual follow-up.
